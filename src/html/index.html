About

Around a year ago, I was introduced to the world of computer programming through my previous career, sign language interpreting. After I was done working as an interpreter for the day, I was given a tour of the development shop and my tour guide explained to me all that they do. I was fascinated. I have always been a lover of computers and a tinkerer, but I hadn't thought about this world since I was a child and made my first online blog using HTML in the nineties.

This experience prompted me to learn more online via Treehouse, Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp. I fell in love with programming. After a while, I felt that I could not learn as quickly as I wanted without more direct guidance, so I signed up for the twelve week intensive Front-End Engineering Program at the Iron Yard Academy in Atlanta. Having completed the course, I am proud of my accomplishments, yet still I am very curious, excited and have many more questions about this work.
